ROBOT

Robots are programmable machines capable of carrying out complex series of actions automatically. They range from industrial arms in factories to autonomous rovers exploring Mars, and are increasingly integrated with Artificial Intelligence (AI) to learn, adapt, and perform a wide variety of tasks with little to no human intervention.

Key Components of a Robot


Most robots consist of three essential elements:

  • Mechanical Construction:

    The physical structure (arms, wheels, gears) that allows movement and interaction with the physical world.

  • Electrical Components:

    Actuators, sensors, and power supplies that provide the energy and sensory feedback needed for operation.
